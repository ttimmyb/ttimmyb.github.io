Second Harvest Heartland Design #1

As Design Team Lead and contributor for a volunteer recruitment campaign with Second Harvest Heartland, I created 5 reusable promotional graphics to help food banks across Minnesota attract more volunteers, specifically bilingual individuals. I determined our team should utilize Canva for ease of client accessibility and reusability. I applied industry-standard design principles including clear visual hierarchy, whitespace, symmetry, and a welcoming brand kit which included typography, and a unified color scheme which would serve to unify the team's designs. This graphic centers around a smiley face formed by the campaign message, “Be the familiar smile someone needs today,” designed to be welcoming and inclusive to all audiences. The circular layout guides the viewer's eye, while the call-to-action and URL are prominently placed to drive engagement. This design balances emotional appeal with functionality and versatility, aligning with the goal of the campaign.
