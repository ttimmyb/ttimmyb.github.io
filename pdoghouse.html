doghouse concept

For this personal branding project, I designed and animated a visual identity for my creative label, “doghouse”, serving as both a learning opportunity and a branded showcase piece. As the sole designer, I conceptualized and executed the full workflow using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects. The design process began with selecting a custom typeface that reflects the rebellious, and misfit tone of Doghouse. I then applied 3D extrusion and beveling techniques to convert the logo into a chrome-like form, introducing realistic materials and textures to enhance dimensionality and surface complexity. In After Effects, I animated the piece with a dynamic "dotty" glow effect to create a futuristic, motion-enhanced signature tailored for digital platforms. Targeted toward audiences immersed in skateboarding, snowboarding, and alternative subcultures, the visual language rejects flat, traditional aesthetics in favor of a bolder, more kinetic presence. The animation is optimized for web and social media use, helping establish brand identity while demonstrating skills in typographic transformation, 3D design principles, and motion graphics for visual storytelling.
